José Rizal’s Birthday

José Rizal, the national hero of the Philippines, was born on June 19, 1861 in Calamba, Laguna. He was a writer, doctor, and nationalist who fought for Filipino rights under Spanish rule.

Who is José Rizal?

Rizal was a brilliant reformist and the founder of La Liga Filipina, an organization that called for peaceful reforms. His novels, "Noli Me Tangere" and "El Filibusterismo", exposed Spanish oppression. Due to his activism, he was exiled to Dapitan and later executed in Bagumbayan (now Rizal Park) on December 30, 1896.

His legacy continues to inspire Filipinos to value education, freedom, and national identity.
